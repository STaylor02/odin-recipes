Hamburger

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling!

This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no
tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

I served this recipe a month ago, and it was very good. We're doing it
again tonight.

Ingredients

  • 31 1/2 lbs Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Onion, Finely Chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Oz Dry Onion Soup Mix
  • 1 Clove Garlic, Minced
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Dried Parsley
  • 1 tsp Dried Basil
  • 1 tsp Dried Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Crushed Dried Rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  2. Meanwhile, combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix,
    minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley,
    basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands
    to form the mixture into 4 patties.

  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center
    and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read
    thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
